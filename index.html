About Youdon Designs

Tenzin Youdon, a fashion designer deeply committed to preserving Tibetan culture through sustainable fashion, founded Youdon Designs.

Our mission is to promote cultural heritage and sustainable practices while supporting the empowerment of Tibetan women.
